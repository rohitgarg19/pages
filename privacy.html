Overview

This privacy policy applies to all mobile applications developed by Rohit Garg. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Our applications do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your privacy is fully protected.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any information. Our applications are designed to function entirely offline and locally on your device. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage data or analytics are gathered
  • No location data is accessed
  • No device identifiers are collected
  • No cookies or tracking technologies are used

Data Storage

Any data you create or input within our applications is stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to this data, and it is never transmitted to any external servers.

  • All data remains on your device
  • No cloud synchronization or backup to external servers
  • Data is removed when you uninstall the application

Third-Party Services

Our applications do not integrate with any third-party services that collect data. This includes:

  • No third-party analytics (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • No advertising networks or ad tracking
  • No social media integrations that track users
  • No crash reporting services that collect device data

Children's Privacy

Our applications are safe for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any data, there are no special concerns regarding children's privacy. We comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) by default through our zero data collection policy.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over any information stored locally on your device through your device's standard data management features.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. Our commitment to not collecting user data will remain unchanged.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our applications, please visit our homepage for contact information.
